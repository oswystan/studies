# working flow

- choose a subject
- collect related books
- choose audiences
- make skeleton
  - background
  - what is it
  - functionalities
  - history
  - conceptions in it
  - how to use it
  - best practices
  - differences to other competitors
  - theories used
  - references
  - index of key words
  - errata
- make a catalog
- make a schedule
- fill contents
